**7.3.33-0 / 7.4.26-0**

- PHP 7.3.33 , 7.4.26
- Apache 2.4.51
- MariaDB 10.4.22
- Perl 5.32.1
- OpenSSL 1.1.1l (UNIX only)
- phpMyAdmin 5.1.1

**Additional information**

- Maintenance release
